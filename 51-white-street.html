51 White Street

Overview

DAB Investments is currently underway on the development of 51 White Street in Tribeca, NY. The 100 plus year old landmarked building features a cast iron marble façade and is remnant of a grander architectural period in NYC history. The building will be meticulously restored to its former glory. Additionally, DAB won the rights from Landmarks to construct an additional crowning two floor penthouse that will sit atop. The building will have some of the highest level of finishes, materials, and designs seen in this neighborhood.
